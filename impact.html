Core results

The platform demonstrates working transport-layer continuity, wireless activity, application-layer visibility, and communication across connected devices.

Continuity behavior

MPTCP was demonstrated as part of the continuity workflow, supporting resilient session behavior across changing network conditions.

Wireless platform activity

Amarisoft logs and end-device interaction show that the network environment is functioning with observable attach and signaling behavior.

Operational awareness

TAK and WebTAK provide a shared live view for monitoring devices, locations, and communication across the network.

Amarisoft device and attach activity

The Amarisoft platform shows connected-device interaction and signaling activity within the wireless testbed.

Result Wireless platform evidence

TAK system integration

Overall impact

The PSBN Prototype combines continuity concepts, wireless infrastructure, and operational applications into one integrated platform.

Working wireless environment

Devices successfully connect and interact within a controlled PSBN testbed.

Visible network behavior

Logs and dashboards provide direct evidence of signaling, sessions, and system health.

Operational functionality

TAK services support real-time visualization and communication between connected endpoints.
