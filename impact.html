Impact & Alignment with PSBN Principles

This prototype evaluates how a resilient PSBN architecture can meet Canada’s ten foundational principles including interoperability, equitable service, affordability, sustainability, coverage, mission-critical delivery, uninterrupted access, security, resiliency, and efficient spectrum use.

Key Impact Areas

  • Continuous connectivity during disasters
  • Secure real-time drone video transmission
  • Priority access during network congestion
  • Improved rural and remote communication reliability
