Planned evaluation metrics

Metrics are selected to match the project goal: determine whether sessions remain usable through disruption, and quantify recovery behavior once testing begins.

Session continuity

Whether a TCP session remains usable across link loss and path transition events.

Recovery time

The time required to return to stable communication after a disruption event.

Stability under change

Throughput/latency behavior during transitions and under degraded path conditions.
