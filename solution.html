Workflow overview

The methodology is structured as a repeatable pipeline: configure interfaces, enable continuity, then prepare controlled disruption scenarios for evaluation once the platform is complete.

1
Configure dual-band connectivity
Bring up LTE and 5G interfaces, confirm independent connectivity, and document baseline network behavior (addressing, routing, and stability).
LTE5GRouting
2
Enable MPTCP
Enable MPTCP support on the Linux-based gateway environment and confirm subflow establishment across both links.
MPTCPSubflowsContinuity
3
Prepare disruption scenarios
Define controlled events such as link loss, path degradation, and handover transitions to evaluate how sessions behave.
OutageHandoverDegradation
4
Collect evidence and publish
Once testing begins, evidence will be published as short videos and clear visual summaries (plots/tables) on the site.
VideosPlotsReporting

Planned scenarios

Scenarios are designed to demonstrate continuity behavior clearly. Final scenario details will be published as testing is completed.

Link loss during active session

Disable LTE or 5G while a TCP-based workflow is active and observe session behavior.

Handover transition

Trigger a path change and observe session stability, recovery behavior, and interruptions.

Path degradation

Reduce bandwidth / increase latency on one path and observe traffic shift and continuity impact.
