Abstract

This capstone investigates a dual-band Public Safety Broadband Network (PSBN) prototype that uses Band 14 LTE and n77 5G alongside Multipath TCP (MPTCP) to evaluate whether TCP sessions can remain usable during disruptions such as link loss and handovers. Evidence and results will be published as testing is completed.

What we’re building

Dual-band connectivity with a transport-layer continuity approach (MPTCP) for TCP-based workflows.

What we’ll evaluate

Outage events, forced handovers, and degraded path conditions during active sessions.

What we’ll publish

Videos + diagrams/photos + simple plots/tables for continuity-focused metrics.
