Public Safety Broadband Network Prototype

This research project investigates the design and implementation of a resilient Public Safety Broadband Network (PSBN) prototype aligned with Canada’s national PSBN principles.

By leveraging dual-band cellular infrastructure (Band 14 LTE and Band n77 5G) and Multipath TCP (MPTCP), the project evaluates continuous connectivity, failover performance, and mission-critical reliability under simulated emergency conditions.

Core Objectives

  • Implement dual-band redundancy
  • Enable seamless failover using MPTCP
  • Test performance under failure conditions
  • Align with Canadian PSBN principles
